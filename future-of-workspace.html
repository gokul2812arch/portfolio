Alpha Link
Human WorkForce has now evolved to Generation Alpha. Progress in AI, Robotics, digital technology has now manifested a future where we walk hand in hand with technology, optimizing our daily lives to an extent beyond imagination. Living side- by the side with robots played an important role in expanding our influence beyond Planet Earth. Hence, we envision a life where opportunities are unlimited; work is as easy as breathing, and innovation has no stops. A concept for skyscraper design utilizing the natural organic growth of unitary cells, which cluster, grow, and evolve to form colonies vertically and applied to the vertical nature of skyscrapers. By creating a lattice structure to which these cells can attach, the organic germinating nature of city growth is reproduced. The clustering of individual cells forms sky-pod colonies, becoming “neighbourhoods” or “buildings” in a vertical urban environment. Thus, the life and energy of the two-dimensional/horizontal urban fabric of the city are continued along on the three-dimensional/vertical axis through the void/core of a transparent and environmentally permeable structure. This allows for the formation of an open-air 360-degree vertical urban corridor. This concept takes skyscraper design from the vacuum of visual icon to the experiential level of the vertical street.
ADAPTATION AND ZONING
Just as a plant thrives in its own particular niche, different building types will colonize the facets of the structure according to their needs. Residences, with higher heating needs, face southward, as offices, with demand for even light and lower cooling loads, tend to the North. As a self-sustaining colony, every liability becomes an asset, every waste, reused. The district systems serve to balance any excess that optimal orientation and passive systems cannot.
COMMUNITY AND OPEN SPACES
The lattice structure which is open, is transformed to form the “plazas” and ”side streets” where communities can interact as well as provide open-air circulation between the sky-pod colonies. This also brings “to light” the isolated nature of city dwelling in typical buildings, in which individual levels do not interact with one another. By providing the visually apparent nature of an open vertical urban core within the skyscraper, community identity is reinforced.
ADAPTATION
Growing and evolving, each individual can take advantage of the unique niches of vertical real estate created in the building . At a scale accessible to the individual vertical farmer, robot polisher, or attorney, the lattice provides framework which can support the smallest entrepreneur or largest corporate entity. The design of the infrastructure suggests a dynamic environment where spaces for social interaction is established in several pockets throughout the building. The spatial structure of the building is designed to be flexible and mobile. Modules within the complex can move and. The frame structure remains a constantly growing and developing spatial complex.
CIRCULATION TUBES
This public transportation system is engineered to transition from underground, to a vertical orientation. This alleviates the need to stop, find parking and/or change into the secondary mode of vertical transportation such as elevators. All other infrastructure is designed to “seamlessly” transition from a horizontal into a vertical system. Escalators, walkways, ramps, bridges and elevators are exposed to facilitate the notion of a vertical urban environment which aesthetically and functionally form the “stitching” between sky pod colonies
Q/A - Alpha Link
Human Work Force has now evolved to Generation Alpha. Progress in AI, Robotics, digital technology has manifested a future where we walk hand in hand with technology. optimizing our daily lives to an extent beyond imagination. Hence, we envisioned an office space where opportunities are unlimited, work is as easy as breathing and innovation has no stops.
1)Are there humans in 2040?
Yes, there are humans in 2040. They look, feel and act the same way as a millennial. Just a bit more crazy, a bit more surgically enhanced. By 2040, every human would be retrofitted with holo eye lenses that would act as AR (Augmented Reality) personal assistants. Other bionic enhancements would include brain enhancements, hearing devices and computer chips. These mechanical addition would automatically set humans on a course towards cyborg life.
2)Do we need to work in 2040?
I wish I could say that we would be able to be lazy and consequence free, but that future is a blue moon away. Work is something what would always be a part of human life till the time there would be a need to evolve. In 2040, work would be so well integrated with our lifestyle that it would be synonymous to breathing. Hence it is important to make it comfortable and easy. Our building gives the user complete freedom not only in the choice of place but also the dimensions of their work place. The malleable nature of space also facilitates an ever-altering and ever-growing commerce industry.
3)Who is my boss in 2040?
Every task needs a leader for proper execution. In an age where optimization is critical and the standard norm, different leaders would be assigned according to the nature of tasks at hand. Yes even in this age hierarchy is imminent. Competence of a person in a task will define his status in a work place irrespective of his age and this is liable to change every day with tasks. We have achieved a fluid arrangement of work spaces to accommodate such hierarchy.
4)Is my workplace secure?
Over the past few years food riots have become more frequent and violent in nature. Here, at Alpha Link, we promise complete user safety. The building safeguards it's users against bio terrorism and food riots through its unique system of adaptive fortification walls. There are multilevel Alpha Links (high speed transport tunnels) that directly connect the inhabitants to surrounding buildings. There is end to end safeguarding systems to ensure a bright hazard free life.
5)Where will I live in 2040?
In the building itself. The future societies would be based on a lifestyle model called INO where specialized pivotal evolution would take humans with creative inclement to the top of the socio-economic class. Work and leisure would have no boundary in between successively it would make little sense to demarcate living and working areas. The structure of the building is made up of pods and voids. The voids transform to form recreational areas such as plazas, greens and streets, they become nodes of interaction between the residents. A key feature of this building is the complete city experience that is provides from isolated pods to interactive public areas.
6)What is a company?
Each building is a city in itself. Every function of a city is mirrored and reworked to adjust it to the current building requirement. Basic needs such as maintenance, administration, energy generation and services would be provided by the building itself. These facilities are executed and monitored by an official body that would work under the owners of the building. This system would be termed as the Company. Each company is a mixed used development that along with work sustains it's economic needs by charging for living subscriptions for self employed citizens, and other business entities
7)Why do we need a subscription model?
The company has dedicated spaces and residences for its employees which are well equipped with high level facilities and upgradations. For freelancers, the subscription determines the size of the pods they inhabit and its expansion possibilities. The co-working spaces they will use would also be customized and details based on their selected living package. Features such as possible orientation, placement and interiors would be available on premium categories only. Spaces can be rented hourly, daily monthly or yearly basis.
8)Who is eligible to subscribe for an office space?
Anyone who can afford it, is free to stay at the Alpha Link. The building was designing as a central hub for alpha generation creative minds. The more the merrier. There is no discrimination against race, ethnicity, gender preference or career choice.
9)Is my office connected to outer space?
Although Space elevator is a reality now, unfortunately it is not feasible to give a physical transport channel to outer space. The company has yearly raffles for trips to the space hotel and there is always direct communication channels to hotel in question. The building does provide roadways and alpha tube connections to surrounding communities. It also has a strong telepathic network system in place. The building has provided for installation of pod stations when flying cars become economically viable.
